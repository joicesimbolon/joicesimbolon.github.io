About Unai

Unai is an evolution of Elucidata Limited. A bespoke software consultancy focused on taking complex problems involving enormous databates and creating value for those seeking clarity and insights.

Unai has built a team of UK based engineers to tackle your software challenges. We have built expertise, in the management of complex, sensitive and often personal data. We have a clear mandate to project the rights of individuals, whilst creating valuable insights from collection of large databates from multiple sources.
